FAQs

Question? Look Here

We specialize in Pakistani, Continental, Italian, and BBQ cuisine made with fresh ingredients and authentic flavors. Our diverse menu offers something for every palate, from traditional Pakistani dishes to international favorites.

We are conveniently located in the heart of Dubai, easily accessible by car or public transportation. Our exact address and directions can be found on our contact page, and we offer valet parking for your convenience.

We are open seven days a week from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Our kitchen serves food until 10:30 PM, and our bar remains open until closing time. We also offer special extended hours during weekends and holidays.

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially for dinner service and weekends. Reservations ensure you get the best table and avoid waiting times. You can book online through our website or call us directly.

Making a reservation is easy! You can use our online reservation form on this website, call us directly, or visit us in person. Simply select your preferred date, time, and number of guests, and we'll confirm your booking within minutes.
